# Privacy Policy

Effective date: 8 July 2026

Durak for Android works entirely on the user's device. It does not require an account, display advertising, use analytics, access the internet, or collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data.

Game statistics and preferences are stored locally on the device using Android application storage. They can be removed by clearing the application's data or uninstalling the application. Depending on the user's Android backup settings, the operating system may include application data in a device backup controlled by the user.

The application does not request sensitive permissions.

Questions about this policy may be sent to [aleksey@karakuts.com](mailto:aleksey@karakuts.com).
